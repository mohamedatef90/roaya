## FAQ Section

### Headline: "Quick Answers to Common Questions"

**Q1: How quickly will I hear back from you?**

A: For sales and general inquiries, we respond within [XX] business hours (typically within [X] hours). For technical support (existing clients), our 24/7 team responds within [XX] minutes for critical issues.

---

**Q2: Do I need to know my exact requirements before contacting you?**

A: Not at all. Many clients contact us with general challenges like "our email is unreliable" or "cloud costs are too high." Our team will ask the right questions to understand your needs and recommend appropriate solutions.

---

**Q3: Will I be pressured into a sales pitch?**

A: Never. Our first conversation is about understanding your challenges and whether Roaya IT is the right fit. If we're not the right solution for you, we'll tell you honestly. We're interested in long-term partnerships, not one-time sales.

---

**Q4: Can I see pricing before I contact you?**

A: Yes! We're the only IT provider in Egypt with transparent public pricing. Visit our [Pricing Page] to see Starter, Professional, and Enterprise tiers for all services. You can also use our [ROI Calculator] to estimate costs and savings without contacting us.

---

**Q5: Do you offer free consultations or assessments?**

A: Yes. Initial consultations are always free with no obligation. For certain services (like cloud migration or SAP assessment), we offer free or low-cost assessments to help you understand your current environment and requirements.

---

**Q6: What information should I prepare before scheduling a call?**

A: Helpful to have:
- Current IT challenges or goals
- Approximate company size and number of users
- Current IT infrastructure (if known)
- Budget range or timeline
But don't worry if you don't have all this—we'll work with whatever information you have.

---

**Q7: Can existing clients use this form for support?**

A: For faster support, existing clients should use our 24/7 support hotline or email support@roaya-it.com. However, you can use this form for non-urgent inquiries or account questions.

---

**Q8: Do you offer support in Arabic?**

A: Yes! Our entire team is fluent in both Arabic and English. You can communicate in whichever language is most comfortable for you.

---

**Q9: What if I'm not in Egypt—can you still help?**

A: While we're based in Egypt and specialize in the Egyptian market, WorldPosta's global infrastructure allows us to serve clients across the Middle East and beyond. Contact us to discuss your location and requirements.

---

**Q10: How do I know if Roaya IT is right for my business?**

A: Great question! We're ideal if you:
- Want transparent pricing and guaranteed ROI
- Need 24/7 local Egyptian support
- Value business outcomes over technology buzzwords
- Want enterprise-grade infrastructure without enterprise complexity
- Are frustrated with your current IT provider

If that sounds like you, let's talk.

## Content Notes

**Form Strategy:**
- Keep required fields minimal (name, email, company, interest, message)
- Optional fields help us provide better service but don't block submission
- Clear labeling with placeholders for guidance
- Progressive disclosure (don't overwhelm)

**Multi-Channel Approach:**
- Form (asynchronous, low-pressure)
- Phone (immediate, personal)
- Email (professional, documented)
- Chat (quick questions, high-value)
- Office visit (high-trust, serious prospects)

**Response Time Emphasis:**
- Clear promises build trust
- Differentiate from slow competitors
- Show we respect their time
- Set expectations appropriately

**Egypt Focus:**
- Emphasize Egyptian team and office
- Arabic language support highlighted
- Egypt phone numbers prominently displayed
- Office location with map (physical presence)

**Reduced Friction:**
- Offer ROI calculator for self-service exploration
- Transparent pricing link (see before contacting)
- Free consultation (no risk)
- No obligation quotes
- Clear privacy policy

**Existing Client Consideration:**
- Separate support contact info
- Client portal access
- Don't force clients to use sales form for support

**Trust Building:**
- Privacy policy linked
- Response time promises
- Team availability clearly stated
- Multiple contact options reduce friction

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Multiple CTAs throughout
- Different paths for different buyer stages (research vs. ready)
- FAQ reduces common objections
- Social proof (response times, client count)
