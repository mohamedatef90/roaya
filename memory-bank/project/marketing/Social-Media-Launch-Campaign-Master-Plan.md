# 4. User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

## Epic 1: Campaign Foundation & Launch

### Story SMC-001: Pricing Transparency Approval (P0 - Blocker)

**User Story:**
> As the **Sales Director**, I want **executive approval on pricing transparency** so that **the marketing team can proceed with campaign execution without legal/business risk**.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
```gherkin
Given pricing transparency is our core differentiator
When the CEO reviews competitive analysis and ROI projections
Then CEO provides written approval to publish pricing OR
Then CEO provides alternative differentiation strategy

Given pricing is approved
When marketing prepares launch materials
Then all pricing content is reviewed by legal/finance
And pricing matches actual service costs
And pricing includes disclaimer for custom enterprise solutions
```

**Business Rules:**
- Pricing must be reviewed quarterly
- Enterprise pricing remains "Contact Us"
- Starter and Professional tiers have fixed published pricing
- Pricing cannot be lower than cost + 30% margin
